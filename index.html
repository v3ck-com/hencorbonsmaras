About Our Farm

Hencor Bonsmaras is a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to the breeding of high-quality Bonsmara cattle. With decades of experience, we are committed to scientific breeding principles, focusing on fertility, adaptability, and profitability. Our mission is to provide fellow farmers with genetically superior animals that will enhance their herds and improve their bottom line. We believe in the power of the Bonsmara breed and strive to uphold its reputation for excellence.
